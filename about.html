Our Mission

At Fur-Ever Mates, our mission is simple: to rescue, rehabilitate, and rehome dogs in need. We are committed to providing a safe haven for abandoned, neglected, and abused dogs, offering them a second chance at life. Our goals include:


  • Rescue: We actively rescue dogs from dire situations, including those surrendered by their owners, abandoned, or in high-kill shelters.
  • Rehabilitate: Our team provides medical care, training, and socialization to help dogs overcome past traumas and become well-adjusted pets.
  • Rehome: We work diligently to match each dog with a loving family, ensuring a perfect fit for both the dog and their new owners.

Through community education and advocacy, we aim to promote responsible pet ownership and reduce the number of dogs needing rescue.
