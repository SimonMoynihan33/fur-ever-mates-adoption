Our Mission

At Fur-Ever Mates, our mission is simple: to rescue, rehabilitate, and rehome dogs in need. We are committed to providing a safe haven for abandoned, neglected, and abused dogs, offering them a second chance at life. Our goals include:


  • Rescue: We actively rescue dogs from dire situations, including those surrendered by their owners, abandoned, or in high-kill shelters.
  • Rehabilitate: Our team provides medical care, training, and socialization to help dogs overcome past traumas and become well-adjusted pets.
  • Rehome: We work diligently to match each dog with a loving family, ensuring a perfect fit for both the dog and their new owners.

Through community education and advocacy, we aim to promote responsible pet ownership and reduce the number of dogs needing rescue.

How can you help?

There are many ways you can support Fur-Ever Mates in our mission to give every dog a loving home:


  1. Adopt: Consider adopting one of our wonderful dogs. By giving a dog a home, you are making space for us to rescue another in need.
  2. Foster: Open your home temporarily to a dog in need. Fostering helps dogs adjust to a home environment and provides valuable insights into their personalities.
  3. Donate: Your financial contributions help cover the costs of medical care, food, and other necessities for the dogs in our care. Every donation, big or small, makes a difference.
  4. Volunteer: Give your time and skills. Whether it's walking dogs, helping at events, or assisting with administrative tasks, your help is invaluable.
  5. Spread the word: Follow us on social media and share our mission with your friends and family. Raising awareness is crucial in finding homes for our dogs.
